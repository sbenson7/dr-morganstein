About

Psychologist, PsyD, MSEd

Dr. Penni Morganstein

Dr. Penni Morganstein provides individual and group counseling, workshops, and administrative consultation on a University level at The New York School of Interior Design and Sotheby’s Institute of Art Masters Programs. In addition, she meets with patients privately in downtown Brooklyn and the Murray Hill neighborhood of Manhattan. Dr. Morganstein earned her PsyD from Pace University, a Master’s in Education from Hunter College and was a Fellow at the Institute for Educational Leadership at New York University.

For more than 30 years Dr. Morganstein has trained and worked in hospitals, schools, Universities and non-profit settings including Long Island College Hospital, Metropolitan Hospital, Mt. Sinai Medical Center and the New Rochelle, NY Public School System. Prior to becoming a psychologist, she taught children and adults, and served as a director of non-profit programs.
