Welcom to the Aesthetic

DR. PENNI MORGANSTEIN provides individual and group counseling, workshops, and administrative consultation on a University level at The New York School of Interior Design and Sotheby’s Institute of Art Masters Programs.

In addition, she meets with patients privately in downtown Brooklyn and the Murray Hill neighborhood of Manhattan. Dr. Morganstein earned her PsyD from Pace University, a Master’s in Education from Hunter College and was a Fellow at the Institute for Educational Leadership at New York University.
